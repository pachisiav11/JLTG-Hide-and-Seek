1 · Install the APK

  1. 1Tap Download the Android app above (on your phone).
  2. 2Open the downloaded JLTG-debug.apk. Android will ask to allow installing from this source — tap Settings → enable Allow from this source, then go back and tap Install.
  3. 3Open JLTG H&S from your app drawer. It needs a network connection to load — it's a thin shell around the live web app, so it's always up to date with no reinstall needed for future web-side changes.

2 · Turn on background alerts

The app has two proximity alerts that matter most while you're not looking at your phone — a hider drifting near the edge of their zone, and a seeker closing in. Both silently do nothing until three Android permissions are granted, and Android buries all three:

📍 Location — “Allow all the time”Not “while using the app”. Background alerts read your position with the screen off; “while using” stops the moment you lock the phone.
🔔 Notifications — allowedWithout this, alerts are computed but never shown.
🔋 Battery — exempt this appSo your phone's battery manager can't pause the alert service while it's in your pocket.

The app walks you through all three itself: open the ☰ menu → Settings → 📘 Guide — the "Background alerts" section detects each grant live and links you straight to the right settings screen. Reopen the Guide after granting one to see it flip to ✅.

3 · How to play

  • Zones — overlay a place's official boundary as a reference, then draw, import, or combine your own play area.
  • Questions — Radar, Thermometer, Matching, Tentacles, Measuring; each shades the map down to where the hider can still be.
  • Stations — lock in the board's stations once; long-press one to add a note or eliminate/restore it.
  • Live location share — the HIDER generates a 4-digit code and reads it out, the SEEKER types it in; the seeker streams position, the hider sees a live red dot plus the two alerts above.
  • Full walkthrough for every tool: ☰ menu → Settings → 📘 Guide, right inside the app.
